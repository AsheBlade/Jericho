## Overview

记录并收藏吃过的印度食物. 


## 待吃清单

大部分是AI推荐

### Chicken Tikka Masala

特点：

烤鸡块 + 番茄奶油酱

香料味浓但辣度可调

西方国家最受欢迎的印度菜之一。

点餐：
“Chicken tikka masala, not spicy please.”

### Palak Paneer

特点：

菠菜咖喱 + 印度奶酪

奶香味

通常不辣

口味比较清爽。


## 绝品

绝品的定义是适合中国/日本人口味的食物. 自己非常喜欢会反复吃, 并且会强烈推荐给朋友和家人. 

### veg uthappam

D18 veg uthappam

Veg uttapam is a popular South Indian thick, savory pancake made from **fermented rice and lentil batter** (or semolina), topped with **finely chopped vegetables** like onions, tomatoes, carrots, and capsicum. It is a nutritious, gluten-free breakfast or snack often served with coconut chutney and sambar. 

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/pw/AP1GczPkjO85JAQwCBKtAcs47SnxVX-VP4PGZmoHgMpS4mf42xjs1A5HRxY3ZaazHuR_H1YckVLC_qaDkRZ0JpGZHsL2kQYVEDdo3s4BhbKaU3B-Qm-I3Su3MaTOy8Xda0Nnzp3IBDGh_RrnWKpO0BnsmfNEVA=w1705-h1279-s-no-gm?authuser=0)

点评: 用米做的蔬菜pancake. 为数不多不带一丝辣味的印度菜. 营养健康, 老少皆宜. 

### butter masala chicken

通常简称为 butter chicken. 鸡肉是通常的做法, 但不一定要放鸡肉, 餐厅一般也提供羊肉和素食的选项. 

Butter Masala Chicken (Murgh Makhani) is a popular, creamy Indian dish featuring **grilled chicken simmered in a silky tomato, cashew, and butter-based gravy**. Originating from Delhi, it’s known for its mild, rich flavor enhanced by kasuri methi (dried fenugreek leaves). The dish is typically served with naan or rice.

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/pw/AP1GczPsI69KhOtUDfr8RqEI-jtNBTYKYOipwS2A54I42418Lv3tpBxPhyiUl8FyvfEk9kOXcR5xGsPI9ai9Upa9N2SS6minxxR43CDBYs7gLB3uheaQZn6U5xgCQoFl36tynM4IHCC0RDXbuLAq-I1Om_ievw=w1706-h1280-s-no-gm?authuser=0)

点评: 印度名菜.  奶油 + 番茄酱汁, 微甜. 口味丰富, 却能做到基本不辣, 雅俗共赏.  适合所有第一次吃印度菜的人. 之前欢送同事离开的时候就去的这家, 大部分白人同事都是点这个. 

点餐可以说：
“Butter chicken, mild please.”

### Parotta

Parotta (or paratha) is a popular, flaky, layered Indian flatbread made from flour (whole wheat or refined), water, and oil/ghee, known for its soft, buttery texture and crispy layers, similar to puff pastry. 

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/pw/AP1GczPNcS6urh0FKxC7rzXEQR1USUDNEiEHwf5M4KjdLYUirycdwtSs7AtJm3wYudY8umu9bLZXKKCP1tgfKL8BysWSqDAs1oA_HIKRy7g-i8pasIr-1Cq41X_ny1TBP0_XGSu32fxyM9RefF47nzJ9JGhlbA=w1706-h1280-s-no-gm?authuser=0)

印度版螺丝饼. 可以单点, 也可以搭配curry 做combo. 鸡肉和羊肉选哪个不重要, 重要的是最好无骨, 要强调不辣. 有骨头吃的太麻烦. 

炖肉配烙饼. 很符合中国人口味的饮食. 



### Garlic Naan

印度的 naan 都好吃



## 好吃

好吃的定义是会吃第二次, 而且会推荐别人吃. 

### Masala dosa

D2 masala dosa

Masala dosa is a popular South Indian breakfast, consisting of a **crispy, fermented rice-and-lentil crepe** stuffed with a savory, spiced potato filling (aloo masala). Traditionally, it is served hot with coconut chutney, tomato chutney, and sambar. Key ingredients include rice, black gram (urad dal), potatoes, onion, curry leaves, mustard seeds, and turmeric

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/pw/AP1GczM-4194Rt5MsFb5f09gy1JreVj4DTk8kAgZCHfDmM3MP-YdLxfAZHHc0sdOY_Bd2Sd_gqEZ9iIIVXULG6E0hreKPRExc4s1A7QVqgvBFn7AA5r7d-ajeQ5rtUNKGNxXx_4wJhXEAxBZSCb9qU_Rp6NC4w=w1705-h1279-s-no-gm?authuser=0)

点评: 好像很多国家都有煎饼果子, 中国有, 法国有, 印度也有.  米做的脆皮煎饼, 里面夹土豆.  煎饼还挺好吃的, 但不是很喜欢这个土豆. 土豆是辣的. 

### Chicken Malai Kabab

Chicken Malai Kabab is a popular, silky-textured Indian and Pakistani kebab made from marinated chicken, often ground or in small pieces, blended with cream, yogurt, and spices like ginger, garlic, and cilantro, giving it a **rich, creamy, and tender flavor**. 

有时也称作 Reshmi Kabab 或者 Creamy Chicken Kebab. 

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/pw/AP1GczNU8T8AgqVF0JZ5W_6fgLM1ZP0E2_uhWcDFP8TsMiTeTqpbYEI7LzB_i9E_eAGI6p9qUUkl3pw596oUY82j-RM6mXs5PNAFgw_u4RkJJsqm1oCDZTxgzAIFCzpBSGFB9Vbk3P7Ctp3Em2pUZd3NvQ3qGQ=w1706-h1280-s-no-gm?authuser=0)

鸡胸肉串. 优点是口感嫩, 无骨, 不辣. 缺点是没什么亮点. 难怪是巴基斯坦食物, 更多伊斯兰风格. 

### Tandoori Chicken

TA6 Tandoori Chicken

Tandoori chicken is a popular Indian dish of chicken marinated in yogurt and spices, traditionally cooked in a tandoor (a cylindrical clay oven) at high temperatures, giving it a smoky flavor. The **marinade, often including ginger, garlic, and chili powder**, gives the chicken its signature red color and tender, juicy texture. While traditionally cooked in a tandoor, it can be replicated at home using an oven, grill, or air fryer. 

上菜的时候是铁板, 还带着嘶嘶声.  [视频](https://photos.app.goo.gl/2vyCDX235V3XnsaXA)

点评: 挂炉烤鸡. Tandoori 是指那个烤的技法, 不是一种味道. 印度很多肉类菜品都是腌制之后呈现红红的颜色.  还可以. 下次有机会尝试一下无骨的版本. 

### Lamb Seekh kabab

TA7 Lamb Seekh kabab 

Lamb seekh kabobs are a classic, savory Indian appetizer made from finely **minced lamb**, seasoned with ginger, garlic, **green chilies**, cilantro, and spices like garam masala. The mixture is molded onto skewers and grilled over hot coals for a smoky flavor, or pan-fried, offering a juicy, tender, and spiced texture.

![](https://lh3.googleusercontent.com/pw/AP1GczO6xkxkJgr4y13q_meCy2AWF6VzbXQlJ3vteClTBv7S5J3efrA6bFUPMGuShDkwjV3pnZwc7zZbSG_Y359w_zJGQA3CSpWyiiQretCJhQMJVZnLhpwWiWVeBEfCNWePHOc9BvZ84hYKfCsZcqImFoLbjQ=w1705-h1279-s-no-gm?authuser=0)

### Filtered  coffee

Filter coffee, also known as pour-over or drip coffee, involves passing hot water through ground coffee in a filter (paper, cloth, or metal) to create a clean, aromatic brew. It is generally characterized by a medium grind size and a brewing time of 2-3 minutes, offering a less intense, lighter-bodied flavour compared to espresso.
